The Introduction

The application is used by the referee coaches to coach and to assess Touch referees.

It contains a referees database. The offline mode permits to work without data connection.

Sensistive data are protected and only reachable by its author, but the data sharing permits a progressive coaching.

  • Documentation
Features

All required features to coach referes during tournaments

Coaching a game

The app permits to take note about the referee performance : the positive points and the axis of improvment. Up to 3 referees can be managed when you are coaching a game.

PRO database

The PRO (Problem Remedy Outcome) feature offers a way to not rewrite the same comments when you are coaching. You can save a full feedback point with the definition of the problem, the remedy and the outcome. By the way you build your own database of PRO.

Assess a referee

You can assess a referee to the referee level. The app includes the assessment sheets of Europe, Australia and New Zealand by default.

Offline mode

The app is designed to be used on tournament on a mobile device. It supports offline mode without data connectivity. Data are cached on your device when you switch to offline mode. Synchronisation is automatic when you switch on.

Referee database

The app integrates a referee database that you can extend at any moment. The database is shared between the referee coaches.

Data privacy

By default your data (coaching note, assessmenet, PRO lib) cannot be seen by the other coaches. However you can decide to share a coaching to another referee coach.

Assess a referee coach

You can assess a referee coach to the coaching level from Europe.

Experience record

The application provides a feature to record your coaching time. This will help the referee coach to build his annual activity report.
